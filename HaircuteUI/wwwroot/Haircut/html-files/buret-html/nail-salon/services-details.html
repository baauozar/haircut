Nail Care & Extension

nail extension

Nail extensions are a popular service offered at nail salons, providing clients with longer,stronger, and more aesthetically pleasing nails. Offering nail extensions can attract clients seeking both functional and fashionable nail solutions, making it a valuable addition to any nail salon's service menu.

Types of Nail Extensions :
  • Acrylic Nails
  • Gel Nails
  • Silk Wrap Nails

Aftercare Tips Regular Maintenance: Schedule regular fills every 2-3 weeks to maintain the integrity and appearance of the extensions.
